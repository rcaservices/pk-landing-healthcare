Policy only. PolicyKeeper is designed for compliance policies, employee handbooks, scope-of-practice documents, and protocols. It is not an EMR and should never be used to store PHI.

Will my staff actually use it?

If they can text, they can use PolicyKeeper. Plain-English questions, no menus, no app to install. The interface is closer to a group chat than to a clinical system — intentionally low-friction.

Is this a HIPAA compliance tool?

No — it's a "your facility's policy" tool. PolicyKeeper answers from your uploaded HIPAA policies and handbook, not from regulatory websites. Where your standards exceed HIPAA, your stricter language is what your staff sees.

PolicyKeeper only answers from documents you've uploaded, and every answer cites the source section. It doesn't make things up and it doesn't pull from the internet. If a policy doesn't cover the question, PolicyKeeper says so.
